Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Personal Customs Clearance Code (PCCC)? expand_more
A PCCC is a unique 13-digit code issued by Korea Customs Service. It's used instead of your resident registration number for personal imports to protect your privacy.
Does the limit include shipping costs? expand_more
For duty-free thresholds ($150/$200), the value is generally based on the price of the goods. However, if you exceed the limit, taxes are calculated on (Price + Shipping + Insurance).
Is a gift subject to tax? expand_more
Yes, even if marked as a "Gift," items are evaluated based on their market value. If the value exceeds the duty-free limit, taxes will apply.
